If you're wondering what to do next, here are some popular next steps, resources and tools.

Courses

There's more to offer from Code First: Girls! Check out codefirstgirls.org.uk and sign up to the newsletter to hear about what's going on.

Meetups and mentoring

  • London Tech Meetups - lists meetups in London, with sections for beginners & underrepresented folks in tech
  • Codebar - special shout out to Codebar (listed on London Tech Meetups) who offer free mentored coding sessions for underrepresented folks in tech - lots of CF:G students have found these a helpful place to go after CF:G

Online resources

  • Free Code Camp - free online coding courses and community
  • Glitch - online code editor which allows you to ask for help with your code online
  • Code Newbie - community and podcast for people learning to code
  • Even longer (open source) list of free resources

Career help

  • The Inclusive Tech Community at Twitter offer CV clinics and mock interviews - DM us for more information
  • CoderList help self-taught developers put together a portfolio and get their first jobs

Keep in touch! Your instructors are likely happy to help out and answer questions after the end of the course.
